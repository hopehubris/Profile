About

I take messy, inherited situations — fragmented tooling, underperforming vendors, ungoverned tech functions — and create clarity, accountability, and momentum.

Most recently, I led LinkedIn's global Technology Support and HR Engineering organizations: 55+ engineers, 120+ managed service staff, 30+ offices across four continents, and a $20M vendor portfolio spanning HCL, Infosys, and Accenture. Before that, Netflix — where I helped build the internal platforms that supported the company's leap into original content and global expansion.

I've also led in contexts most technology leaders haven't: a university in the UAE for seven years, a global ed-tech network spanning 60+ institutions and 600,000 students, an aerospace manufacturer, a telecom giant mid-spinout. That breadth gives me pattern recognition that's hard to fake — I've seen the same transformation challenges play out across radically different industries, cultures, and resource environments.

I care deeply about the human side of technology leadership: how you build teams that own their systems, earn trust from skeptical business leaders, and create cultures where engineers solve problems rather than close tickets.
